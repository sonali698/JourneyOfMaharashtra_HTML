Konkan

Konkan, a beautiful place which is as good as a paradise, a coastline at western part of India. The sandy golden beaches with crystal clear ocean is the main attractions for all the travelers across the world to visit Konkan at least once in a life time. Konkan beaches are not typical beaches which are mostly crowded. You will find peace and relaxation on many of the Konkan beaches.

But Konkan is just not all about the beaches. Konkan Tourism has many more attributes that everyone ought to know about.

How To Reach Konkan?

To reach Konkan you can travel by road transportation, airways and by Konkan railways. I would recommend everyone to travel by Konkan railways. You will have a great experience of your life by watching lush full greenery, deep valleys and refreshing air.

While traveling by Konkan railway, you are certainly going to be very impressed by the mountain ranges and green carpet of trees and villages. Konkan railway passes through some breath taking modern tunnels and bridges, which are also major highlights when you travel by Konkan railway.

The city is well linked with all major cities in India by rail and road.
