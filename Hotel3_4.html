Swaminarayan Residency

Book This Hotel

Hotel Description

Swaminarayan Residency is one among the most affordable budget properties situated in Nashik. Decked with modern-day amenities and high-end comforts, this hotel is extremely hospitable. Situated in the heart of the ever-bustling city, this hotel offers a perfect ambience to leisure and business travelers to relax. The well-experienced staff at this hotel offers full assistance to its guests along with top- class facilities. Travelers who wish to explore Nashik can book a room in advance at this hotel. Apart from well-furnished rooms, the hotel also has a banquet hall and lush lawns that host social gatherings and celebrations.

The in-room facilities of this property include comfortable bed, make-up mirror, wardrobe, refrigerator, Cable television, electronic safe, tea/ coffee maker and an attached bathroom with hot and cold water. Swaminarayan Residency also offers its guests with ample parking, and Wi-Fi facility in public area. Doctor on call, laundry, car hire and postal & courier services are few other services that this property offers. The hotel also features a travel desk that is adept in providing best travel assistance to first timers. Swaminarayan Residency also houses an in-house restaurant which specializes in serving delectable pure vegetarian food to its guests. If you are looking for a delightful hotel that suits your pocket, then Hotel Swaminarayan Residency will be a great choice to make.
