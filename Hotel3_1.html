Hotel Rama Heritage

Book This Hotel

Hotel Description

Hotel Rama Heritage is one among the most preferred properties situated in Nashik. The hotel offers top class services and accommodation to avid travelers. Easily accessible and centrally located, the hotel is decked with handpicked modern day amenities to facilitate its guests. The staff at Hotel Rama Heritage is heartwarming and extremely ever-welcoming. Offering round the clock services to its guests, the staff here makes the hotel stand out from the rest. This property offers ample parking, and verdant gardens offering the ultimate ambience to lay back. Travelers who wish to wander past Nashik, can best book their rooms in advance at this hotel.

The rooms at this hotel are well furnished with the much needed amenities like a comfortable bed, make-up mirror, wardrobe, a seating area, flat screen TV and an attached bathroom with hot and cold water running. Wi-Fi facility is also available at this hotel which also houses conference and banquet halls. Hotel Rama Heritage serves pure-veg dishes which are great to relish. Laundry services and doctor on call are few other facilities that the hotel offers to its much valued guests. As this hotel is well-equipped with desktops, printer and high speed internet, it also serves as an ideal business centre. While you enjoy your stay at this aesthetically designed hotel, you can also indulge in a sightseeing trip to few of the most popular and spiritual attractions of Nashik, namely Ramkund and Kapleshewar Mandir.
