Hotel Description

Hotel Siddharth Inn is a 3 star property which offers excellent hospitality to its guests. Especially designed to meet the requirements of corporate and business travelers, the property is centrally located. A host to large gatherings, celebrations, parties, banquets and conferences, this hotel features a capacious conference and banquet hall. The rooms of this hotel are elegantly designed and are adorned with modern amenities offering utmost comfort. Travelers who wish to explore the scenic Nagpur, can book their stay in advance at this hotel. The staff appointed here is heart-warming and ever-welcoming and offers full assistance to its guests.
