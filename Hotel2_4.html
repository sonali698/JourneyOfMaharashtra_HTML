Hotel Description

Hotel Woodlands is an ideal property for business and leisure travelers. Adorned with elegant rooms and contemporary amenities, the hotel offers an extremely pleasant stay to avid travelers. Situated close to Nagpur Railway Station, the hotel provides a diverse range of services to its guests. Featuring elite rooms, the hotel also houses a multi-purpose hall decked with indispensable amenities. Perfect for hosting social or corporate events, this property also excels in offering utmost comfort and relaxation to its guests. Offering hospitality since 1978, the hotel has a well-experienced and heart-warming staff, always present to offer full time assistance. Travelers wishing to explore the captivating locales of Nagpur, can book rooms in advance at this hotel.
