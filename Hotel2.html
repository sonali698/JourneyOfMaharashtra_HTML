Hotel Description

Hotel Grand Mehfil having been in existence from 3 decades is one among the pioneers in hospitality. Featuring up-class amenities and a commendable infrastructure, this property hosts great family events, public celebrations and business activities. The hotel is easily accessible and is a classic place to stay in. An extremely relaxing ambience coupled with a welcoming staff, makes it an ideal crash pad for avid tourists. Travelers who wish to explore Amravati, can best book a room in advance at this hotel, which offers a perfect space to rejuvenate.
