Aurangabad

Home to ancient and spectacular Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Aurangabad is the perfect city to cherish the rich history of Maharashta. The city's winsome land is also dotted with palaces, tombs, and parks, making it a must visit tourist spot for history and architecture buffs. Aurangabad also boasts an opulent collection of handicraft items and metal goods that make excellent souvenirs to carry back home.

How To Reach Aurangabad?

Aurangabad is a distinct city in the Aurangabad district of Maharashtra. A noted tourist spot owing to umpteen historical and cultural places to visit, this city has a prompt connectivity to all parts of India and abroad. all modes of transport like air, rail and road are available in Aurangabad, which you can opt as per your convenience. Here is a brief picture of how you can reach this historical city of India.

BY AIR:

Aurangabad Airport, also known as Chikkalthana Airport has well connected flights to Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Udaipur, Pune, Jaipur, and Nagpur.

BY RAIL:

Aurangabad has its own Rail head on the Secunderabad-Manmad section of South Central Railway in Indian Railways (Nanded Division). The city has rail connectivity to Delhi, Chennai, Ahmedabad, Mumbai (Fastest - Aurangabad Jan Shatabdi Express), and Hyderabad. It also has trains connecting to Nizamabad, Nagpur, Shirdi, Visakhapatnam, Nanded, Parli, Nasik, Amritsar, Kurnool, Pune, Ambala, Kakinada, Renigunta, Madurai, Gwalior, Vadodra, Bhopal, Narsapur, Rameswaram, Tirupati, Okha and Rajkot.

BY ROAD:

The major cities of Maharashtra and other states are continually connected by buses to Aurangabad. The prime roadway is the National Highway 211 that starts from Dhule up to Solapur and passes through Aurangabad. It has excellent road connectivity to Pune, Jalna, Ahmednagar, Beed, Nagpur and Mumbai. The recently developed four lane road of National Highway is presently the busiest route for these buses. Nagpur-Aurangabad-Mumbai express highway is still under construction. Central Bus station and CIDCO bus station are the prime bus stations in Aurangabad.
